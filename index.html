Our Love Story

Our paths first crossed at a mutual friend's birthday celebration in Victoria Island, Lagos. Chloe was captivated by Richard's warm smile and infectious laughter, while Richard was drawn to Chloe's grace and intelligence. What started as a simple conversation about Nigerian music and culture turned into hours of deep connection.

Three years later, Richard proposed during a romantic sunset dinner overlooking the Lagos lagoon, with traditional Yoruba and Igbo blessings from both families. Now, we're excited to celebrate our union with all our loved ones in the beautiful tradition of Nigerian weddings!
